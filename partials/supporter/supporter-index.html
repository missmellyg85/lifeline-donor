Our Services
Pregnancy tests, limited obstetrical ultrasound, prenatal & parenting education, peer counseling & abstinence education are just a few of the services Lifeline is able to provide to our clients completely free of charge, thanks to the generous support of our community.

Our Team
The Lifeline team, comprised of our Staff, Board of Directors, and Volunteers connects our community's support directly to our clients' various needs.

Our Community
The very foundation of Lifeline's ministry is our community of supporters - individuals, churches, families, organizations & friends whose partnership in giving allows for the daily work and growth of Lifeline's ministry.
