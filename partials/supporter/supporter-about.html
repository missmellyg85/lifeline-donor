About Us

Lifeline Pregnancy Help Clinic is a non-profit, Christ-centered organization that upholds the value of human life through the care of pregnant women and those affected by an unplanned pregnancy. We exist to save and change lives.

Lifeline was established in 1997 and since has been serving women and families from all of Northeast Missouri. Our service areas are broad, but our overall goal is the same: to serve all with an open heart and open arms as they progress through pregnancy and into parenthood.
