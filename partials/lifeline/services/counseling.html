Peer Counseling

With everything you have to think about, you may feel like no one understands your situation right now. Lifeline is here to listen without judgment. You can meet one-on-one with a peer counselor who will help you process information, your feelings, and the choices ahead. The decision of how to handle your pregnancy is up to you; Lifeline wants for you to be able to make that decision confidently, and with our understanding and support.
